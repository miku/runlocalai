# Misc

* [https://huggingface.co/spaces/tventurella/mr_chatterbox](https://huggingface.co/spaces/tventurella/mr_chatterbox)

> Mr. Chatterbox is a language model trained entirely from scratch on a corpus
> of over 28,000 Victorian-era British texts published between 1837 and 1899,
> drawn from a dataset made available by the British Library. He is not a
> modern AI putting on an accent — his vocabulary, ideas, and worldview are
> formed exclusively from nineteenth-century literature.

> He excels at discussions of Victorian life, literature, science, philosophy,
> manners, and the great questions of the age. Ask him about the railways, the
> Crystal Palace, Mr. Darwin's theories, or the proper conduct of a gentleman.
> As the model is still in beta, some responses may be a little wonky. If this
> happens, click on an answer to regenerate it.
